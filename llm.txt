## About Unicus Vision
Unicus Vision delivers advanced machine vision inspection, track & trace, serialization, and quality control systems for manufacturing industries. We provide end-to-end solutions that combine state-of-the-art cameras, intelligent sensors, and AI-powered software to detect defects, verify labels, read codes, and ensure product integrity on high-speed production lines.

## Core Products & Services

### Inspection Systems
- Label Inspection Systems - detect misalignment, missing labels, print defects, and quality issues at production line speeds
- Print Inspection Systems - automated detection of print defects, color variations, and registration issues
- Code Readers - high-speed barcode, QR code, and data matrix verification with OCR and OCV
- Soap Inspection Systems - specialized systems for embossing, surface defects, and packaging quality control

### Track & Trace Solutions
- End-to-end traceability systems for product visibility and supply chain compliance
- Serialization systems - generate, print, and verify unique identifiers for compliant track and trace
- DSCSA-compliant solutions for pharmaceutical manufacturers
- UDI-compliant systems for medical device manufacturers
