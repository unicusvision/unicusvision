How regulated industries accelerate compliance and global operations with Unicus Vision

Pharmaceutical

Clinical & Regulatory Document Processing

A global pharma company needed to digitise and translate thousands of clinical trial reports, batch records, and CTD/eCTD submissions for multi-country regulatory filings.

  • AI-powered OCR with medical terminology recognition
  • ISO 17100-certified human-in-the-loop translation
  • Automated redaction of PII & sensitive data
  • Direct export to eCTD submission portals
Result: 60% faster regulatory submissions; $1.2M/year saved in manual translation & filing labor
Manufacturing & Engineering

Multi-Language SOP & Technical Manual Management

An industrial equipment manufacturer required rapid translation and digitisation of safety manuals, CAD documentation, and standard operating procedures for 15+ global markets.

  • Layout-preserving translation (PDF/InDesign/FrameMaker)
  • Terminology consistency via custom translation memory
  • Automated version control & change tracking
  • Integration with PLM & DMS systems
Result: Zero compliance gaps across regions; 85% reduction in manual document update cycles
Logistics & Trade

Cross-Border Customs & Compliance Documentation

A global logistics provider needed automated extraction and translation of bills of lading, customs declarations, and certificates of origin to accelerate cross-border shipments.

  • Intelligent data extraction from scanned/faxed forms
  • Real-time translation with trade-specific glossaries
  • Automated validation against HS codes & regulations
  • Secure cloud repository with role-based access
Result: 40% faster customs clearance; 99.8% data accuracy reducing shipment delays & penalties
