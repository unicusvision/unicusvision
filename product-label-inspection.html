Real-World Use Cases

See how leading manufacturers achieve zero-defect labeling with Unicus Vision systems

Pharmaceutical

Serialization Compliance for Blister Packs

A global pharma manufacturer needed to verify unique serial numbers, expiry dates, and batch codes on high-speed blister packaging lines while meeting EU FMD and DSCSA requirements.

  • OCR/OCV verification of variable data
  • 2D code grade validation (ISO 15415)
  • Label presence & position check
  • Real-time rejection of non-compliant units
Result: 100% regulatory compliance achieved with 0.2% false reject rate at 400 packs/min
Food & Beverage

Allergen Label Verification on Flexible Packaging

A food producer required 100% verification of allergen statements, nutritional info, and multi-language labels on flexible pouches running at 300 units/minute.

  • Multi-region text verification
  • Color contrast validation for readability
  • Label skew & wrinkle detection
  • Integration with MES for audit trails
Result: Zero allergen mislabeling incidents post-installation; 30% reduction in manual QC labor
Cosmetics

Premium Brand Protection on Luxury Packaging

A luxury cosmetics brand needed to ensure flawless label application, print quality, and hologram verification on high-value products to protect brand integrity.

  • High-resolution surface defect detection
  • Hologram authenticity verification
  • Gold foil & embossing quality check
  • Color consistency monitoring (ΔE < 1.5)
Result: 99.98% first-pass yield; eliminated $2.1M/year in brand damage from labeling errors
