Real-World Use Cases

How leading brands maintain print quality excellence with Unicus Vision

Food Packaging

Flexible Pouch Print Quality Control

A snack food manufacturer needed to detect ink smears, color shifts, and misregistration on high-speed flexible pouch lines running at 400 pouches/min.

  • Multi-camera array for full surface coverage
  • Spectral color analysis (Lab color space)
  • AI-powered defect classification (smear vs. scratch)
  • Automatic press adjustment feedback loop
Result: 92% reduction in customer complaints; $450K/year saved in waste and rework
Pharmaceutical Labels

Regulatory-Compliant Label Printing

A pharma company required verification of critical text (dosage, warnings) and barcodes on labels while meeting FDA 21 CFR Part 11 documentation requirements.

  • OCR verification of all human-readable text
  • Barcode grade validation (ISO 15416)
  • Electronic audit trail with image capture
  • Role-based access for quality approvals
Result: Zero labeling recalls post-installation; 100% audit readiness for regulatory inspections
Luxury Cosmetics

Premium Brand Protection on Packaging

A luxury brand needed to ensure flawless print quality on foil-stamped cartons, detecting micro-defects invisible to the human eye.

  • High-resolution imaging for micro-defect detection
  • Gold foil coverage & consistency verification
  • Embossing depth & clarity analysis
  • Color consistency monitoring across batches
Result: 99.95% first-pass yield; eliminated $1.8M/year in brand damage from packaging defects

Technical Specifications

Engineered for high-speed, high-accuracy print inspection

📷

Imaging System

Line-scan or area-scan cameras; 4K–16K resolution; up to 80 kHz line rate; synchronized multi-camera arrays

💡

Lighting Solutions

Programmable LED arrays: dome, coaxial, backlight; multi-wavelength (RGB, UV, IR); strobe sync for motion freeze

🎨

Color Analysis

Spectrophotometer-grade color measurement; ΔE calculation in Lab, LCh, CMYK; tolerance-based pass/fail

🧠

AI Processing

Deep learning models for defect classification; on-edge inference; continuous learning from operator feedback
