Real-World Use Cases

How manufacturers achieve serialization compliance with Unicus Vision systems

Pharmaceutical

DSCSA Unit-Level Serialization

A pharma manufacturer needed to serialize millions of saleable units monthly while maintaining line speeds of 500 units/minute and meeting DSCSA verification requirements.

  • GS1-compliant SGTIN generation with cryptographic signing
  • High-resolution inkjet/laser printing on diverse substrates
  • Real-time 2D code verification (ISO 15415 Grade A)
  • Automatic database sync with repository systems
Result: 100% DSCSA compliance achieved; 0.05% false reject rate at 500 units/min; $2.8M/year saved in manual processes
Food & Beverage

Batch Traceability for Recall Management

A food producer required unique batch serialization on flexible packaging to enable precise, rapid recalls while meeting FDA FSMA requirements.

  • Dynamic code generation linked to batch/lot data
  • Flexible material printing (film, foil, paper)
  • OCR verification of human-readable batch codes
  • Integration with quality management systems
Result: Recall execution time reduced from 48hrs to 20min; 95% less product waste during recall events
Cosmetics

Luxury Brand Protection via Serialization

A premium cosmetics brand needed secure serialization to combat counterfeiting while maintaining elegant packaging aesthetics.

  • Discreet, high-density DataMatrix codes on luxury packaging
  • Consumer-facing authentication via mobile app
  • Blockchain-integrated serial number registry
  • Real-time alerts for suspicious verification attempts
Result: 88% reduction in counterfeit incidents; 40% increase in consumer engagement via authentication app
