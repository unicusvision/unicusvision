Real-World Use Cases

How soap manufacturers achieve consistent quality with Unicus Vision systems

Premium Soaps

Embossing & Logo Verification

A luxury soap brand needed to verify intricate embossed logos and text on every bar while maintaining high-speed production of 400 bars/minute.

  • 3D profilometry for embossing depth verification
  • High-contrast imaging for logo clarity
  • AI-powered defect classification (chip vs. scratch)
  • Real-time rejection of sub-standard bars
Result: 99.95% first-pass yield; eliminated $850K/year in brand damage from defective embossing
Mass Production

High-Speed Surface Inspection

A large-scale manufacturer required detection of surface defects (cracks, pits, discoloration) on soap bars moving at 600 units/minute in humid conditions.

  • IP69K-rated cameras for washdown environments
  • Polarized lighting to eliminate soap glare
  • Multi-angle imaging for 360° coverage
  • Edge computing for sub-30ms decision time
Result: 92% reduction in customer complaints; 40% less waste through early defect detection
Packaging Lines

Wrapper & Carton Quality Control

A producer needed to verify wrapper alignment, seal integrity, and carton labeling on final packaging while tracking batch codes for traceability.

  • Seal integrity verification via thermal imaging
  • Wrapper position & wrinkle detection
  • OCR verification of batch/expiry codes
  • Integration with MES for full traceability
Result: Zero packaging-related recalls; 100% batch traceability for regulatory compliance
