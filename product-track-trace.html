DSCSA Compliance & Product Verification

A global pharma company needed to implement DSCSA-compliant track & trace across 12 manufacturing sites with seamless repository connectivity.

  • Unique serialization at unit, bundle, case, pallet levels
  • EPCIS 2.0 event generation & repository integration
  • Real-time verification at distribution points
  • Automated suspicious product investigation workflows
Result: 100% DSCSA compliance achieved 6 months ahead of deadline; $4.2M/year saved in manual reconciliation
Food & Beverage

Supply Chain Transparency for Premium Brands

A premium food brand required end-to-end traceability from farm to shelf to verify authenticity and enable rapid recall management.

  • Blockchain-integrated serialization for immutable records
  • Consumer-facing QR codes for product journey visibility
  • Automated recall execution with precise batch targeting
  • Supplier portal for ingredient traceability
Result: Recall time reduced from 72hrs to 15min; 35% increase in consumer trust metrics
